About Mumbai

Mumbai is the financial capital of India, known for Bollywood, the Gateway of India, and vibrant street life. It's located on the western coast of the country and is one of the most populous cities in the world.

Famous Places

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus
  • Elephanta Caves
  • Juhu Beach
